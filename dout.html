OUT REACH

The Outreach initiatives of the Department of Machine Learning aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and societal needs. Through various outreach programs, students actively engage with communities, schools, and organizations to apply their technical knowledge for social good.
These activities include workshops, awareness programs, training sessions, technical assistance, and community-based projects. Outreach programs help students develop leadership skills, social responsibility, teamwork, and real-world problem-solving abilities while contributing positively to society.
The department strongly believes that education should not remain confined to classrooms, but should extend beyond the campus to empower communities and promote inclusive growth.

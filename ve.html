Value Education and Personality Development

  1. Credits : 2
  2. Outcomes :
    • CO1: Students will be able to Differentiate Accepted norms and Counter values and be able to identify the various Dimensions of Human Development.
    • CO2: Students will be able to Demonstrate Love and Experience of God and identify the Basic issues of Life and Happiness as a life goal.
    • CO3: They will able to Understand the importance of Concern for others and critique the various problems that deter the growth of the society. engine.
    • CO4: The students will be able to Recognize the traits of a good personality and practice Self exploration.
    • CO5: Students will be able to Interpret the Purpose of Life and Goal Setting and demonstrate Self-management.
